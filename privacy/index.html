What data do we collect?

Our Company collects the following data:

  • •.Your name 

  • •.Your email address 

  • •.Any session-specific information you provide when scheduling for a tutoring session. 

  • •.Metadata sent to our server by your browser 

How do we collect your data?

You directly provide the website with all of the data we collect. We collect data and process data when you:

  • •.Visit any page on ryantorok.net 

  • •.Register for a tutoring session 

  • •.Have another user register for a tutoring session on your behalf 

  • •.Update an existing tutoring session 

  • •.Pay for a tutoring session 

How will we use your data?

Our Company collects your data so that we can:

  • •.Serve our content 

  • •.Process tutoring registrations 

  • •.Display a schedule of open tutoring sessions to users. 

 

How do we store your data?

Our Company securely stores your data on a physical server using hardware encryption. The server is designed to only allow your tutoring registration information to be visible to yourself, and, in the case of non-payment-related information regarding your registration, any other students co-scheduled with you in that registration.

Any metadata sent to our server by your browser is temporarily stored, but never in association with other data you provide to us. The data will be deleted within no more than seven days, unless continued storage is required for evidentiary purposes. In which case, all or part of the data will be excluded from deletion until the investigation of the relevant incident is resolved.

Your registration data will be stored until at most 1 hour after either your session’s scheduled time slot ends or we receive confirmation of your payment for the session, whichever is later.

If you cancel a registration, data related to that registration will be deleted immediately. If multiple students share a time slot, any data that is shared between all registrants in the slot will be deleted if and only if the above timing condition is met or all registrants cancel.

If you fail to confirm a registered session, data for that registration will be deleted at most 5 minutes after the 10-minute confirmation window ends.

All data deletions occur by removing the relevant record from our server database, and we cannot recover deleted data.

What are your data protection rights?

We would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

The right to access – You have the right to request us for copies of your personal data. We may charge you a small fee for this service.

The right to rectification – You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request us to complete the information you believe is incomplete.

The right to erasure – You have the right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to restrict processing – You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to object to processing – You have the right to object to our processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to data portability – You have the right to request that we transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at our email: ryan@ryantorok.net
